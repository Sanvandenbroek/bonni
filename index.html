Introduction

We often meet people in the specific roles they play in our lives — a friend, a parent, a neighbor, a colleague. But every person carries so many more identities, woven through time and experience. I realized this while leafing through the photo albums of “Bonni,” my grandmother, who passed away three years ago. Across more than twenty albums, she captured her memories: from a curious young girl, to a spirited teenager, a woman in her twenties, a first-time mother, a mother of three, and finally “Bonni” — the grandmother of three grandchildren. Through this website, I want to keep her stories alive — to celebrate the many lives she lived, and to give her memories a new home in the present.
